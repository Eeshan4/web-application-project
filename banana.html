Banana Chips Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Ripe bananas
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Peel the bananas and slice them thinly.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan.
  3. Add the banana slices to the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  4. Remove the chips from the pan and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  5. Sprinkle salt over the chips and let them cool.

Enjoy your homemade banana chips!
