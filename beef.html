Beef Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cover and simmer for 1 hour, or until beef is tender.
