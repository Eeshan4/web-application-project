Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the bacon until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the pan with the bacon.
  5. Turn off the heat and quickly pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring continuously to coat the pasta evenly.
  6. Continue stirring until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
