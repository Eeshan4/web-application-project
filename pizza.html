My Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil in a bowl to make the pizza dough.
  2. Knead the dough until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rise for about an hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  5. Roll out the dough into a round shape.
  6. Spread pizza sauce on the dough.
  7. Sprinkle cheese and your favorite toppings on top.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  10. Slice and serve!
